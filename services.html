What We Do

Workflow Optimization
Workflow is crucial for the smooth operation of any pharmacy. At MB Pharma Services, we specialize in optimizing pharmacy workflows to enhance productivity, reduce operational costs, and improve patient care.

Standard Operating Procedure(SOP) strengthening
Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) are essential for maintaining consistency, compliance, and efficiency in pharmacy operations. At MB Pharma services, we focus on strengthening your SOPs includes: SOP Assessment, Development and Enhancement, Training and Implementation.

Point Of Sales Improvisation(POS)
A well-functioning Point of Sale (POS) system is crucial for managing transactions, tracking inventory, and enhancing customer service. At MB Pharma services, we focus on POS Improvisation includes: POS System Assessment, System Upgrades and Integration, Data Management and Analytics.

Inventory Management
Effective inventory management is crucial for ensuring that your pharmacy runs smoothly, minimizes costs, and meets patient needs. At MB Pharma services, we specialize in providing comprehensive inventory management service includes: Inventory Assessment, Inventory Optimization, Automated Replenishment, Inventory Control Procedures, Supplier Management.

Regulatory Compliance
Navigating complex landscape of regulatory requirements is essential for maintaining compliance and avoiding legal issues. At MB Pharma Services, we specialize in providing comprehensive regulatory compliance services to help your pharmacy adhere to all relevant laws and regulations.
